About BSI

The (BSI) British Stand Institution is the business standards company that helps organisations all over the world to make excellence a habit. For more than a century they have been challenging mediocrity and complacency to help embed excellence into the way people and products work. That means showing businesses how to improve performance, reduce risk and achieve sustainable growth. BSI work with many different industries, businesses, governments and consumers to develop British, European and international standards, which impact on all businesses and industries depending on their need.

What does Team Concept do?

This team has come together to bring an informative welcome pack to life. The aim of the project is to cater for people with disabilities. This website will make an easy approach for users with disabilities to access the information and will also enable routes and methods for them to get to and attend conferences at the BSI Building. We have seen that many websites do not fully support people with disabilities and we want to offer good solutions to making their life easier.
